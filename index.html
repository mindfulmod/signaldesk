SignalDesk

Cross-platform market attention separated from confirmation and crowding risk. Built on free public data from social channels, news, SEC EDGAR, FINRA daily short-sale volume, and public price/volume feeds.

For discovery and research only — not financial advice. No score predicts returns or removes the risk of loss.
